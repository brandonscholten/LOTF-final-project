LOTF Text Adventure!

What was supposed to be an evacuation ended in a plane crash. You find yourself on a large tropical Island. The plane has been decimated but you somehow survived.

While Swimming you come accross an interesting looking shell.

While exploring the Island you come accross a pig which has gotten itself stuck in the brush. You have the sudden urge to plunge your knife into its flesh.

You are drawn back to the beach by the eerie sound of the conch. A group of children is standing in a group, also drawn in by the sound.

It is decided that a meeting should be called to establish order on the Island.

During the meeting a group of hunters is designated. You have the option of joining them.

It is suggested that a signal fire would be nessecary in order for a timely rescue.

After the meeting, everyone gets to work on building a signal fire. Unfortunately, the fire burns out of control. During the chaos you notice one of the younger children has gone missing.

Life on the island is starting to feel normal. Everyone spends their days bathing, building fires, or playing in the sand. Of course there is work to be done to sustain livable conditions, but things are generally good.

You eventually make your way back to the platform. Two shelters have been built and a few boys are sitting around with tired looks on their faces.

The hunt is successful. The pig wreaths on the ground in pain as it is continuously poked and prodded to death by primitive wooden spears. The others begin a chant that sounds almost tribal.

A ship passes the Island, but there was no signal fire to lure in rescue. The hunters come back with their catch. There will be meat tonight, but a rare chance at rescue has been lost. You are furious.

You feel the sting of a hand accross your face and notice your glasses are missing, they must have taken them to re-light the fire. They could have just asked.
